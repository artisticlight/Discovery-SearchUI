Performing Short Baseline Subset (SBAS) Searches

When you perform a search and the results are returned, you can browse the list of resulting scenes in the left column. As you click on the different scenes, you will see the center column reflect the metadata for the scene.

In addition, there are buttons below the metadata. Almost all scenes have a "More Like This" button. If the scene is suitable for interferometry, there will be two additional buttons: "Baseline Tool" and "SBAS Tool". Click the "SBAS Tool" button to perform an SBAS search.
