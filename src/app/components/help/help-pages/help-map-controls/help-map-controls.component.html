Map Controls In-Depth

looks_one
Navigate To Your Area of Interest

Drag the map while holding down the left mouse button to move to your area of interest.

  1. Use the 'Map Projection' buttons in the map toolbar to toggle between the Arctic, Equatorial, and Antarctic views.
  2. Use the 'Zoom' buttons to zoom in and out of the map to clearly see your area of interest.
  3. Use the 'View' buttons to toggle between the satellite and street map views of the map.
looks_two
Draw On The Map

By default, the map-drawing tool is a bounding box. Click on the map once to specify the starting corner, move the mouse, then click again to finish the box.

Use the 'Area of Interest' buttons to toggle drawing on the map on and off, to edit the shape of the bounding box, or to delete the bounding box.

Use the 'Selection Shape' buttons to switch between different drawing shapes including: point, line, polygon, and bounding box.
